PicsArt_06-17-05.50.16.jpg According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Pai village is 058946. Pai village is located in Fatehpur Pundri Tehsil of Kaithal district in Haryana, India. It is situated 8km away from sub-district headquarter Fatehpur Pundri and 22km away from district headquarter Kaithal. As per 2009 stats, Pai village is also a gram panchayat.

The total geographical area of village is 3179 hectares. Pai has a total population of 18,148 peoples. There are about 3,499 houses in Pai village. As per 2019 stats, Pai villages comes under Pundri assembly & Kurukshetra parliamentary constituency. Pundri is nearest town to Pai.
